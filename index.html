FAN FEST

Fan Fest El Salvador, was an idea that began as a result of a passion, Anime and Video Games. In order to carry out a healthy entertainment event for children and adults, it was necessary to organize and build a good work team and have excellent communication in order to not only complete this objective but also offer quality to the attending public. In this event it was not easy to carry out all the content offered, in this case: Videogame tournaments, Concerts, Cosplay Contests, Product Stands and Food. The most difficult thing was to organize the arrival of our Special Guest: Cesar Franco, the Mexican singer of the Openings and Endings Latin version of our Favorite Anime series.
